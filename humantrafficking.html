Human Trafficking Overview

The definition of human trafficking is the illegal trade of human beings. It’s the recruitment, control, and use of people for their bodies and for their labor. It’s the dehumanization of persons through the involuntary trade of their bodies. With the use of force, fraud, and coercion, people everywhere are being bought and sold against their will, even right now in the 21st century. This is the reality: slavery is violence. It’s physical, verbal, and sexual abuse. It’s forced prostitution.
