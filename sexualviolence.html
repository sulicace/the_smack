SEXUAL VIOLENCE


Sexual Assault Overview

Every 98 seconds, another person experiences sexual assault. There are different types of sexual abuse: Child Abuse, Sexual Abuse of Men and Boys, Intimate Partner Sexual Violence, Incest, drug-facilitated sexual assault. The effects of sexual violence include depression, flashbacks, and Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder. When engaging in sexual activity, consent plays a very important role and it should happen every time. Giving consent for one activity does not mean consent for every activity. After sexual assault, you may not know how to react or where to go. A good idea is to talk to someone you trust and go from there.
