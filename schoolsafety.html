School Safety Overview

Unfortunately, there are many violences that occur on school campuses. Recently, school shootings have been happening more frequently. Due to the overwhelming amount of shootings that have taken place across the nation, students have decided to take leadership in this problem and fight for themselves as it seems there is nothing else that they can do. Teens in the United States have started a movement, March for Our Lives in order to increase safety in schools, regulate gun control and fund gun violence research to put a stop to shootings in America’s schools.
