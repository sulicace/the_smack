IMMIGRATION


Immigration Overview

Immigration is the idea that someone goes to live in a country that is not theirs. In order to do this a person would have to cross over to the country illegally and try to live there as an illegal immigrant. A lot of the immigrants move to another country in search of more jobs and a better life for them and their family. Although that is their intention, many people like our president, believe that every immigrant is a criminal, rapist, and or drug dealer. While that is not always the case, innocent people that are just here to give their family a better life are often deported and ripped from their family.





IMMIGRATION STATISTICS



DESCRIPTION:

The majority of people coming into the US and receiving green cards are family members joining either their immediate or extended family. A large number of people are also getting green cards for work purposes. Immigration into the US has decreased since the 1990s.
